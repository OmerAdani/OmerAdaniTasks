What is Passion Fruit?

Passion fruit, known scientifically as Passiflora edulis, is a tropical fruit that boasts a distinctive appearance, captivating both in its vibrant colours and unique shape. The fruit itself is small to medium-sized, with a round or oval shape. Its outer skin is firm and smooth, ranging in colour from vibrant purple or deep maroon to bright yellow. Some varieties even feature a combination of colours, creating a visually stunning exterior.

Health Benefits of Passion Fruit

Passion fruit is a rich source of dietary fibre. Fibre helps in keeping the digestive system properly functioning and is responsible for keeping gut health intact. People with bowel disorders and constipation are advised to eat foods rich in fibre. This is because research proves that fibre helps make the stool softer, allowing for easy bowel movements. Furthermore, studies show that fibre is also beneficial in boosting heart health by reducing cholesterol. Since it doesn’t contain any sterols, it is a great fruit for people who have heart conditions.

Best Way to Eat Passion Fruit

Scoop out the passion fruit pulp with a spoon and place it in a bowl. You can also enjoy passion fruit pulp straight from the shell. All you'll need is a spoon! Try sprinkling a little of your favorite sweetener onto the pulp to cut the tartness.
